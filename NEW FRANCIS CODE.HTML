SAINT FRANCIS OF ASSISI

Early Life


Francis was born in 1181 in Assisi, Italy, to a wealthy merchant family.

His early life was marked by luxury and extravagance

The Call To Serve


In 1204, Francis had a spiritual awakening while praying in the

church of San Damiano. He heard a voice telling him to "repair my church."

The Founding Of The Franciscan Order


Francis renounced his wealth and privilege, and began to live a life of

poverty and simplicity. He attracted a group of followers, and together

they formed the Order of Friars Minor.

Ministry And Miracles


Francis spent his life preaching, teaching, and serving the poor.

He was known for his love of nature and his ability to communicate

with animals.

Death And Legacy


Francis died on October 4, 1226, in Assisi. He was canonized a saint

in 1228, and his feast day is celebrated on October 4th.
